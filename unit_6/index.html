The Age of Flowers

The Age of Flowers a journey through a relationship of a deep lover. Those that are deep lovers understand that the heart is only the start of love, but it actually encompasses the whole being. If you could look back and change the outcome, would you? Can your heart be reincarnated when deep love is lost? Can courage guide the way? And is it wrong to love deeply and beyond what someone else is able to accept?

Elaine had always reserved her heart, but this tall figure had crossed her path and somehow seemed to draw out her heart. Erik saw her in ways that no one else understood her. It was almost that he was even unaware of how much he saw of her, or he never let on to what he felt. Join Elaine as she journeys through what it means to love beyond the boarders of her heart. The Age of Flowers is an experience to be encountered.

About The Author

I enjoy tinkering with stuff, along with being a slight techie and messing with photography when I ever get the chance. I am a lover of life and pursuer of inner healing. There are too many fun things to do in life. I decided not to pick just one avenue, I want to experience life in every way possible.

In life, I have been found to be a lush for love. I have given love whole-heartedly and without reservation, making the conscious decision to not control or manipulate the people, especially in relationships. To the best of my ability and what I knew at the time, I set out to love open handed. Relationships start with beautiful people, and have good intentions. As humans, we get caught up in wanting to be right and not what is best. I got so focused in wanting to make it work, to the point that I was willing to make sacrifices to love and support the beautiful human that was in my hand to love. From this, I have found inspiration to write and share my passion. I have learned through life and hope that my story can give courage love life in a whole new way.
